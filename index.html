How does this even work?
  1. Everyone writes a ridiculous opening phrase.
  2. Your phrase gets handed to the next player, who has to draw it — no words allowed.
  3. That drawing gets handed to someone else, who has to guess what on earth it is.
  4. Guess → drawing → guess, back and forth, until every chain has passed through everyone.
  5. Then everyone watches every chain fall apart from start to finish.

This is a fan-made party game inspired by drawing-telephone games, built for laughs with friends. Anyone with the link can create or join rooms, and everything you type or draw is visible to everyone in your room. Refreshing the page will drop you from your game.
